The Video Workflow

Script to MP4.
Entirely in the app.

1

Write or Paste Your Script

Already have a script? Paste it. Don't have one? The built-in Script Writer builds a full storytelling script from your topic, audience, hook style, and length — all in your saved writing voice.

AI Script Writer built in
2

Select Your Voice — Preview Before You Commit

Browse 30+ voices from Google Gemini TTS, Pollinations Qwen, ElevenLabs, and GenAIPro. Play a sample clip before committing. Add emotion tags — [whisper], [dramatic], [warm] — for expressive storytelling delivery.

Preview every voice before generating
3

Generate Scene Images in Batches

The app splits your script into scenes and writes image prompts automatically. Generate 1, 2, or 4 images at a time. Images stack in a persistent grid and never disappear. Pick your best takes for each scene.

Batch 1 / 2 / 4 images at once
4

Approve Every Scene First

Review every scene image before a single cent of voiceover credit is spent. Regenerate individual scenes. Swap images. Perfect your visual story before assembly begins. This is The Pipeline Philosophy — never waste credits on bad images.

Zero wasted credits
5

Create Your Video or Download for Your Editor

Create Video assembles a finished MP4 directly in your browser using FFmpeg — Ken Burns zoom on every scene, voiceover synced perfectly, no external tools needed. Or Download for Editor gets you pre-cut MP4 clips timed to your voiceover for CapCut, Premiere, or DaVinci — with script, scenes, and prompts saved in the ZIP.

Questions & Answers

Everything you want
to know before joining.

Do I need to know how to code?
+
Not at all. The Orchard is a fully visual app. You type a prompt, upload a reference image, click Generate, and your content is ready. No coding, no technical setup, no developer needed. If you can use Instagram, you can use The Orchard.
What API keys do I need and what do they cost?
+
You only need two keys to run the full app: a Google API key (free from aistudio.google.com — takes 2 minutes) and a Pollinations API key (free from enter.pollinations.ai). Both are free. The Google key unlocks Imagen 4 and Google TTS voiceover. The Pollinations key covers everything else — Claude writing, Flux/Seedream/GPT images, LTX/Wan/Seedance video, ElevenLabs voice, and transcription. Your subscription covers access to The Orchard. The API providers handle generation at near-zero cost per item.
How does "Bring Your Own Keys" actually work?
+
You go to the Workspace Tab inside The Orchard, enter your API keys once, and they are saved to your workspace. Every time you generate an image, video, voiceover, or blog post, The Orchard sends the request to the AI provider using your key. You pay the provider directly at their rates — which are extremely low. A Flux Schnell image costs a fraction of a cent. A 30-second AI video costs about 0.15 pollen — less than a penny. The Orchard never charges you per generation. Your subscription is your only recurring cost to us.
How is the blog feature different from RightBlogger?
+
The Orchard's Blog Pipeline matches RightBlogger feature for feature — draggable outline editor, H2/H3 structure, SEO scoring, WordPress direct publish — and adds things RightBlogger doesn't have: bulk generation straight from your Google Sheets content calendar, YouTube URL to blog post using the free native transcript (no extra API needed), writing style learned from your own scripts, and 40+ language support per workspace. RightBlogger costs $49/month for just the blog tool. The Orchard gives you the blog pipeline plus 9 other tools for $29/month.
Can I make videos without Creatomate or any paid video tool?
+
Yes — completely. The Orchard uses FFmpeg WebAssembly which runs entirely inside your browser for free. You write your script, pick your voice, generate your scene images, approve them, and click Create Video. The app assembles the finished MP4 with Ken Burns zoom effects and your voiceover synced to each scene — no Creatomate, no Runway, no external subscription needed. Or you can click Download for Editor to get pre-cut MP4 clips perfectly timed to your voiceover so you can drop them straight into CapCut with zero trimming. Both options are completely free.
What is the Founding Member deal and when does it expire?
+
The first 100 members who join lock in their pricing for life. Creator Annual is $99/year ($8.25/month). Grower Annual is $169/year ($14.08/month). Once those 100 spots are taken, the price returns to the standard rate. No exceptions. Your founding price never changes — no matter how many features get added, no matter what the product becomes. This is how Apple Tycoon plants seeds with the people who believed first.
Can I use The Orchard for multiple channels or clients?
+
Absolutely. Each workspace is completely separate — its own characters, writing style, API key settings, platform connections, language, and content history. Creator gives you 3 workspaces. Grower gives you unlimited. Run your relationship channel in one workspace, your car channel in another, your client's brand in a third. Everything stays completely isolated. Switching workspaces switches everything instantly.
Why does this cost so much less than competing tools?
+
Because the overhead is near zero. Other tools charge you a premium to cover their API costs. The Orchard operates on a zero-overhead model — you bring your own keys, so your generation costs go directly to the AI providers at their base rates. The Orchard only charges you for access to the studio itself. There is no middleman markup on your generations. That is how we can offer ten complete tools for $29/month while our competitors charge that for one.
